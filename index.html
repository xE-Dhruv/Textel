About

Textel is a free and open-source tool that converts images into ASCII art directly in your browser. Simply upload an image and Textel will transform it into text-based artwork using ASCII characters. No registration is required, and your images are processed locally in your browser for a fast and private experience.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is ASCII art?

ASCII art is a form of digital art created using text characters instead of pixels.

Which image formats are supported?

Textel supports common image formats such as PNG, JPG, JPEG and WebP.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. Images are processed directly in your browser and are not uploaded or stored.

Is Textel free to use?

Yes. Textel is completely free and open source.

Can I download the generated ASCII art?

Yes. You can copy or download the generated ASCII output.

Why does the output look different from the original image?

ASCII art uses text characters to represent an image, so some details may be simplified depending on the selected settings and image size.
